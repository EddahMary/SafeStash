How it works!!

  1. Open www.safestash.com on your browser of choice
  2. On the very top you'll see a button written stores. Proceed to click it
  3. You will be redirected to a page with our different stores owned by our verified store owners where you can choose one and click the store of your liking
  4. You will then see different slots available in your store of choice where you can book one according to the goods you want stored i.e considering factors such as size
  5. On booking you will get an option to either take your goods to the store yourself or request for them to be picked up. You will be provided with a contact for the owner in case of any delays for you to reach out
  6. You can then get your goods back anytime by navigating to the slot you booked and either request for delivery or choosing to pick up the goods yourself. You'll also be able to see how much you have to pay for the time period your goods have been stored for you.
